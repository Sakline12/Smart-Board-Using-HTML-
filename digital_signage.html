Floor Standing Network Digital Signage

Projecting digital images, videos, web pages, weather data, restaurant menus, and texts – our electronic displays revolutionize wayfinding, exhibitions, marketing, and outdoor advertising. Able to be used in public spaces, transportation systems, museums, stadiums, retail stores, hotels, restaurants, and corporate buildings – the displays can be managed centrally and controlled individually when it comes to exhibiting text, animated and video messages for advertising, information, entertainment, and merchandising to targeted audiences.

Auto play and loop play after power on, videos switching within zero second, without black screen.

Background server management software, set parameters, make playlist and split screen files. Download video resources in real time, auto play when download completed

Video and picture can be rotated 90°/180°/270°. display purposes

Built in clock IC, auto correct system time. Auto turn on/off at the specific time. Save energy, extend service life.

Display product information in form of text when playing video ads..

Support up to two videos,multiple picture, caption display on one screen, free to split the display area.
